## Segregated Content

Windows are useful to segregate content into a well-defined area in the UI.

## Floating and Docking

Windows usually float.  
That is what they're meant for: flexibly move content across the screen so that you can arrange content as you please. That is how you're accostumed to.

`PacemWindowElement` can be dragged (and rescaled as well) when you put them inside a `PacemWindowAreaElement`.
