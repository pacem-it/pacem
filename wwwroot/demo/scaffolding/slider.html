## Handy
The slider scaffolding element might become handy and elegant at the same time when a numeric form field comes with _min_ and _max_ (range) constraints.  


## Customizable
The slider has several levels of customization:

- **Label formatting**: intercept the `formatvalue` event to change the labels output.  


## Formattable and Autogeneratable
`PacemSliderElement` value can be formatted given straight attribute or relevant metadata.
